JSALT Conversation Evaluation

Listen to each conversation segment and rate both speakers.

Consent

This listening test is part of a JSALT tutorial on conversation evaluation. You will listen to short conversation clips and provide ratings about enjoyment and whether the speakers seemed in sync.

Your responses will be used for tutorial discussion and aggregate analysis. Please do not include personally identifying information in the optional comments. You may stop at any time.

Evaluation Guidelines

Please evaluate the conversation based on the following dimensions.

1. Enjoyment (Speaker A) (Scale: 1 to 9)

How enjoyable did Speaker A seem to find the conversation?

  • 1: Not at all
  • 9: Extremely

2. Enjoyment (Speaker B) (Scale: 1 to 9)

How enjoyable did Speaker B seem to find the conversation?

  • 1: Not at all
  • 9: Extremely

3. Our Thoughts Synced Up (Scale: 1 to 7)

Evaluate the extent to which the speakers seemed to get onto the same wavelength.

  • 1: Strongly disagree ("Their thoughts did not sync up.")
  • 7: Strongly agree ("Their thoughts synced up.")
