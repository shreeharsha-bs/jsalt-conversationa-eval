Evaluation Guidelines

Please evaluate the conversation based on the following dimensions.

1. Enjoyment (Speaker A) (Scale: 1 to 9)

How enjoyable did Speaker A seem to find the conversation?

  • 1: Not at all
  • 9: Extremely

2. Enjoyment (Speaker B) (Scale: 1 to 9)

How enjoyable did Speaker B seem to find the conversation?

  • 1: Not at all
  • 9: Extremely

3. Shared Reality (Scale: 1 to 7)

Evaluate the extent to which the speakers developed a joint perspective.

  • 1: Strongly disagree ("They did not develop a joint perspective.")
  • 7: Strongly agree ("They developed a joint perspective.")
